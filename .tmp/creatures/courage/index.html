Courage

Courage manifests itself into Numenite creatures of greatly flashy natures. They are apt to always be moving, to be boisterous and beligerent, and to make hard decisions. When they are Good, they are motivating, decisive, and adventurous. When they are Bad, they are cocky, violent, and stubborn.
