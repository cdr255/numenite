Numenite

This wiki is a collection of all of the information currently known about the world of Numenite. As we discover more, we will make room and adjust what we have written... but rather than keep the information to ourselves, we wanted to publish it as soon as possible.

Numenite is a very strange place, one fraught with danger for the unaware. Check back early and often, so You can learn all there is to know.

Numenite manifests creatures in one of four known groups:

World

The Known World of Numenite is a large island, with some smaller archepelagos nearby. It is made up of various small tribes of people.
